About LEO Events

LEO Events, an award-winning Tennessee based global events and brand experience agency, provides strategic event solutions to companies like Walmart, AutoZone, and HiltonHotel Corporation since 2012. A WomenOwned Business, LEOEvents has consecutively been honored as a Top 100 Agency on Event marketer’s IT List, a Top 50 Events Company by SpecialEvents Magazine and a Top 200 Event Marketing Agency byChief Marketer.

Dedicated to creating virtual, hybrid, and in-person events for the world’s biggest brands, LEO’s core offerings include strategy, creative, production, meeting services, and entertainment–digital or live. LEO Events–Follow The Roar.
