1: Will I be on camera during the event?

You will not be on camera during the event unless you are one of the presenters or are in one of the breakout rooms.

2: What attire is required?

All attendees should wear business casual.

3: What device or internet requirements do I need for the live event?

Make sure you have access to a reliable Wi-Fi network. We recommend a minimum of 10 Mbps download for the best viewing experience. To test your network’s speed, go to speedtest.net speedtest.net.

4: How do I achieve the best viewing experience?

Use Chrome or Firefox for your browser. Once in session, maximize to full screen.

5: Who should I contact if I have general questions about the live event?

Contact our Manager, Digital Strategy, Dani Martin at dmartin@leoevents.com .

6: How do I submit a question for the Q&A Session?

Utilize the "Submit a question" box located below the livestream. Questions will go to speakers directly and will not be seen by other attendees.

7: Is there a way for me to interact with other attendees?

Yes, utilize the chat feature within the livestream once sessions are live.

8: Are there going to be breaks during the sessions?

Yes, programming breaks will be available throughout the sessions. See the agenda for specific timing.

9: How do I enable my camera?

Make sure you are using a Chrome browser. When you first launch the video chat you will be prompted to allow https://leoeventsvirtua.eventfinity.co/ to use your camera and microphone. Press the “Allow” button.

If you did not receive this pop-up or pressed another button, you can go to the right of your address bar and select the camera icon. You will see a pop-up that allows you to select “Continue” allowing https://leoeventsvirtua.eventfinity.co/ to access your camera and microphone. Make sure that is selected and press done.


10: How do I turn off my audio?

If you wish to turn off your audio, press the microphone button on the left side of the screen. The button will become a grey icon that is crossed out.


11: How do I hide my video?

If you wish to hide your camera, press the camera button on the left side of the screen. The button will become a grey icon that is crossed out.


12: How do I end the call?

To end the call, simply close the page (by pressing ctrl-W or by clicking the “X” in your browser tab.


13: I am getting a pop-up error when I enter the chat room.

Make sure you are using a Chrome browser.


14: Other people in my chat can’t see me.

Make sure your camera features are enabled, there is nothing covering your laptop camera, and you are not sitting in front of a window.
